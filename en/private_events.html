Exclusive Rental of Yamato Restaurant

Treat yourself to an unforgettable evening in complete privacy. You can reserve the entire Yamato restaurant exclusively – just for you and your guests. The minimum consumption required for full exclusivity is 80,000 CZK.

Our goal is to offer you an authentic Japanese gastronomic experience in an intimate and sophisticated atmosphere.

We offer two types of service:

1. Kaishoku – Shared Festive Menu

A traditional Japanese style of serving where dishes are brought to the table gradually and shared among guests. This creates space for tasting a wide range of flavors and cooking techniques.

Sample menu structure:

  • Zensai – Appetizers and small delicacies (Scallops, Kamchatka crab, foie gras, shiitake, and more)
  • Sashimi – Traditional and modern selection of raw fish
  • Yakimono – Grilled delicacies (Wagyu A5+, grilled fish, duck breast)
  • Agemono – Tempura (Fried shrimp and vegetables)
  • Nimono – Simmered dishes (e.g., two-day pork belly “Buta no kaku ni”)
  • Sushi moriawase – Chef’s choice sushi selection
  • Desserts – A mix of sweet endings

2. Kaiseki – Tasting Menu

A formal 7-course menu, served individually to each guest. Emphasis is placed on seasonality, aesthetics, and flavor harmony.

Menu structure:

  • Zensai – Appetizer
  • Otsukuri – Sashimi
  • Yakimono – Grilled course
  • Agemono – Fried course
  • Nimono – Simmered course
  • Sushi – Sushi selection
  • Misoshiru – Miso soup

We will be happy to recommend premium drinks from our exclusive selection of Champagne, fine wines, Japanese sake, and Japanese whiskies. Upon request, we can also arrange specific alcohol brands according to your preferences.

A 10% service charge will be added to the final amount.
