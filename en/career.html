Career at Yamato

Join Our Team

We are looking for a new team member to join us at Yamato as a chef. We’re seeking someone who’s eager to learn, work with high-quality ingredients, and become part of a team united by a passion for Japanese gastronomy, professionalism, and a human approach.

What We Offer:

  • A great team with a friendly atmosphere and supportive leadership
  • Training and ongoing guidance – experience is not required
  • A unique opportunity to learn from a world sushi champion
  • Work in an authentic environment where every detail matters
  • Fair financial compensation and opportunities for professional growth

Who We’re Looking For:

  • A reliable and motivated person with an interest in gastronomy
  • A team player who respects the craft and enjoys learning new things
  • Kitchen experience is an advantage, but not a requirement – we’ll be happy to teach you everything
