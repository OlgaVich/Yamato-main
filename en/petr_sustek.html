Assoc. Prof. JUDr. Petr Šustek, Ph.D.

Czech lawyer, attorney, and university lecturer. Head of the Department of Health Law at the Faculty of Law, Charles University in Prague. Specializes in civil and health law, and is a member of expert committees at the Czech Ministry of Health.
